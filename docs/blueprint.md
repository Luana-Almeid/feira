# **App Name**: Feira Livre Manager

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Create and manage a catalog of available products with details like name, purchase price, and selling price.
- Purchase Tracking: Log new purchases, track quantities, purchase price, and date of purchase from CEASA.
- Sales Recording: Record daily sales transactions, track items sold, selling price, and payment method.
- Inventory Management: Automatically update inventory levels based on purchases and sales, providing real-time stock status.
- Waste Tracking: Record spoiled or discarded products, specifying quantity and reason for disposal, helping identify potential waste reduction strategies.
- Smart Order Recommendations: Leverage historical sales data and current inventory to provide tool for intelligent recommendations on quantities of each product to purchase from CEASA, aiming to minimize waste and maximize profit.
- Reporting & Analytics: Generate reports on sales trends, popular products, waste analysis, and profit margins, aiding in data-driven decision-making.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Fresh lime green (#A3D957), evoking a sense of natural freshness and vitality, contrasting against a light background.
- Background color: Soft beige (#F2F1E6), providing a neutral and clean canvas that enhances readability and visual comfort.
- Accent color: Zesty orange (#E07A33), drawing attention to key actions and elements, such as CTAs and important data points.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a clear and modern sans-serif, provides a balanced and readable feel across all text elements.
- Use clear, minimalist icons related to inventory, sales, and produce, ensuring easy recognition and intuitive navigation.
- Emphasize a clean, grid-based layout with intuitive navigation to ensure a user-friendly experience. Implement card-based design for product listings and reports for easy readability.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and animations, such as fade-in effects and progress indicators, to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
